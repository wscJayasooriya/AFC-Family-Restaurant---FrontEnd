Our History

AFC Family Restaurant is new and most famous restaurant in Karapitiya, Sri Lanka. Started in 2017 by a family of Abeywickrama Foodcity (pvt) ltd.

Our Services

AFC Family Restaurant has pioneered Cuisine of Western , Italian Cuisine, Thai Noodles, Mongolian Coner , etc.. in Sri Lanka, offering authentic food refined specifically to suit the local palate. Popular innovations have also become a trademark - our Hot Buttered Cuttlefish, Crab Claws, Hot and Spicy Seafood Soup (Tom Yum), Seafood Fried Rice, Chilli Chicken, Chilli Paste and Devilled dishes have become synonymous with Chinese food in Sri Lanka – often imitated but never replicated.

For a taste of the food that you have come to know and love, visit one of our branches or use our online ordering page to experience Sri Lanka’s premier delivery service and enjoy our dishes in the comfort of your own home.
